On a cold winter morning in Madison, Stephen wakes up to find out that communication to the outside world is down and the whole city is now missing. Confused and cold, Stephen sets out to find out what really happened.
Stephen walks out of his house. Alone and in the cold, he makes his way to the convience store in an attempt to find anyone else.
He finds no one, but there is a mysterious warm cheese pizza out in the open.
Who had done it? he thought. There were more surivivors to this catastrophe and Stephen hopes to find others.
